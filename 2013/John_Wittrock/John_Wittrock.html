John Wittrock: CERTOS

CERTOS: CERTOS (Component-Encapsulated Real-Time Operating System) is a novel means to combine the benefits of both real-time and general-purpose operating systems onto a single machine. CERTOS is based on Composite, a research operating system being developed here at GW, and which is a microkernel component-based OS. This allows for interesting interactions between Composite and a real-time OS such as FreeRTOS running on top of it. CERTOS can allow FreeRTOS to still make real-time guarantees, and do dynamic checkpoint and restore of an entire operating system in about 100 microseconds.
