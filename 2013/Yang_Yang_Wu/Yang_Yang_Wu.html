Yang Yang Wu: IsolOS

IsolOS: The purpose of the project is to explore the feasibility of providing isolation between multiple operating systems running under different subsets of processor cores. The project will utilize the Linux OS and Composite OS. We want to allow Composite to run predictably, with respect to real-time systems. To achieve this, we will need to enforce isolation of the two OSes, allthewhile still enabling Composite to leverage the pre-existing functionalities of Linux, such as device drivers.
