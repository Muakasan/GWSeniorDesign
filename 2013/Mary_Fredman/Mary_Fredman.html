GW Senior CS Design 2013

Mary Fredman: MathRomp

MathRomp:

My senior design project, MathRomp, is a mobile social app that turns mathematics into a fun, competitive game. Through the game, users will be able to solve various math problems of different difficulty levels and, subsequently, challenge their friends to solve those same problems. In addition, the app will also provide a platform for teachers and professors to be able to create specific groups of users, their students, and program specific assignments and problem sets for them to complete.
