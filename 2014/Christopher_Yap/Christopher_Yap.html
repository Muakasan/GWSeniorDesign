FocusEye: FocusEye is a system that uses eye tracking software to look at how the movement of one’s eyes affects the ability to learn and retain information. By using the Mirametrix eye tracking hardware and system of focus and learning games, FocusEye will provide users with useful and meaningful data about their own or subjects eye movements and how they correlate with how well subjects are retaining the information presented to them. Further, the program aims to test if a subject can train their focus by incrementing the difficulty of the games and tests. If there is a correlation between eye movement and the retention and information, the program will show this by giving data about how long the subject’s eyes are focused on the material.
