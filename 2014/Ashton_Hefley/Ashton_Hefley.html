GW Senior CS Design 2014

Ashton Hefley: C-Flat

C-Flat: is an educational application designed to teach beginners music theory and composition. Users face the perils of randomly generated questions, composition-related challenges, and a dark, gloomy user interface.

Bio: Music was there for as long as I can remember. Learning songs on the piano was like learning to read. It was there, no matter what else was going on in my life. Not everybody has this opportunity to develop a rich friendship with music. I want to extend this opportunity to those less fortunate across the world and to establish the importance of music education across the States. Oh, and I love kids. I teach them reading and math every saturday with the For Love of Children organization.
