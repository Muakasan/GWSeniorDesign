GW Senior CS Design 2014

Princess Allen: Owl-Sight Secure

Owl-Sight Secure: is a test-monitoring system that insures exam integrity for online test-taking. It will give you a cheat-proof exam-taking environment. Owl-Sight Secure monitors testing irregularities by video and audio through webcam and through disabling external programs to the test.

Bio: I am a student at The George Washington University working towards a a BS in Computer Science with a minor in Criminal Justice. My professional interest lies in the field of cyber security.
