GW Senior CS Design 2012

Avinash Bharatsingh: Maestro

Maestro-Interact With Your Music: Maestro is an interactive music conducting program. Anyone can control any song as a conductor would control the orchestra. This will happen in an authentic conducting environment where the conductor can create their own gestures to conduct the music. They will be able to control the music in the following ways: Volume, Tempo, Pitch, Echo, Flange, Distortion, Chorus, Highpass/Lowpass.
