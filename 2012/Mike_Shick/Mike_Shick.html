GW Senior CS Design 2012

Mike Shick: Roboderp

Roboderp-Scheduling For Robots: Roboderp provides a method for scheduling the tasks of many different autonomous robots.

Bio: I’m a programmer, tinkerer and Free software advocate. I’m happiest when I’m working on an interesting problem or puzzle. My work is primarily in C (GNU/Linux development, OS research) and Java (Android development). I also like rapid development in Python, and have an ever-growing interest in functional programming, and specifically Haskell. For fun, I write Android programs and play Magic: the Gathering. I also love music, movies, and books.
